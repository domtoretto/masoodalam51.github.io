Facebook Related Post Plugin For Octopress Blog

Assalamualaikum

After Trying Many Related Post Plugin for Octopress Blog i Discover this plugin for octopress and this plugin uses facebook apps.Using this plugin post which related by your current post is displayed by facebook.So you can engage your visitor more socially.Its open in left or right corner of user screen with hiding button and like button.User can easily like your content.For checking demo you can see any post of my website.

Tutorial

1.Go to Facebook Developers.

2.Click On Apps Button.

3.If its your first app then its directly open or click on .

4.A new box open where you can fill only your desired name and click continue.

5.After above step a new page open.In Website with Facebook Login Box simply Enter your Octopress Blog Address And click Ok.Dont click on any Extra Button Or Fill any Boxes and copy App Id and keep it.

6.Go to Your Octopress Source > _includes

7.Open head.html and At Line 4 see <head> replace it with <head xmlns:fb="http://ogp.me/ns/fb#">

8.Open facebook_like.html.At Line 7 you see js.src = "//connect.facebook.net/en_US/all.js#appId=212934732101925&xfbml=1"; replace it with js.src = "//connect.facebook.net/en_US/all.js#appId=Your App Id&xfbml=1";

Replace Your App Id With your app id whic is copy in 5th step.

9.Open article.html and after <div class="entry-content">{{ content }}</div> Add Below Code.

Replace http://www.techelex.org With Your Site Name.Set right or left side="right".

Generate your Blog And Deploy It.See Demo At your Own Blog.
